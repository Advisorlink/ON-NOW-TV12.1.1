          Backup & Restore

          Download a complete snapshot of this launcher (dock tiles, wallpapers, every uploaded APK, app store assets, QR videos, V2 AI assets, devices, layout settings) as a single ZIP. Restore it on a fresh launcher in one click to migrate everything across servers — no SSH, no scp.

          Step 1 · Download

          Backup this launcher

          Saves a ZIP with the full /data folder — usually a few hundred MB depending on how many APKs you've uploaded. Use this on your old launcher first.

          Step 2 · Upload

          Restore from a backup

          Upload a ZIP you saved from another launcher. Existing files are overwritten — dock tiles, APKs, icons, layout, devices, everything. Vesper Logins are not affected (they live on the Vesper backend).

          How to migrate from your old launcher

          1. Open your old launcher's admin UI, switch to the Backup tab, click Download backup. You'll get a launcher-backup-YYYYMMDD-HHMMSS.zip.
          2. Open this launcher's admin UI, come back here, click Choose backup ZIP, pick the file you just downloaded, then click Restore launcher.
          3. Refresh the page once it's done — all your old dock tiles, wallpapers, APKs and devices will be here.

          Tip: if your old launcher doesn't yet have a Backup tab, you can hit GET /api/admin/backup on it directly from the browser address bar and the file will download.
